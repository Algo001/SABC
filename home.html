About Us

SABC, the South Asian Bible Church, is a Christ-centered community that prioritizes the well-being of families and stands against persecution. Rooted in the teachings of Jesus, we provide a nurturing environment for spiritual growth and offer support to individuals of all ages. We are committed to upholding the values of love, compassion, and unity in the face of opposition. Through worship, fellowship, and biblical teachings, we equip our members to navigate life's challenges with faith and resilience. Join us at SABC as we embrace Christ, uplift families, and stand firm against persecution, making a positive impact in our communities and beyond.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission is to guide people of all backgrounds into a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.

Vision

Our vision is to build a vibrant, life-giving church community that positively impacts our city and beyond with the love of Christ.
