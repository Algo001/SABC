Our Story

Our church was founded in 1998 by Pastor Salim Arthur. With a small group of dedicated individuals, we came together to worship and serve the Lord. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers, united in our faith and commitment to spreading the love of Christ.

We gather each week to worship, study the Word of God, and support one another on our spiritual journeys. Through various ministries and outreach programs, we strive to make a positive impact on our local community and beyond.

What We Believe In

  • We believe in the Holy Trinity - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and the Savior of the world.
  • We believe in the power of prayer and the importance of cultivating a personal relationship with God.
  • We believe in the authority of the Bible as the inspired Word of God.
  • We believe in the value of community and encourage fellowship among believers.
  • We believe in serving others and spreading the message of God's love.
